We believe that the presence of loved ones can significantly enhance a patient's recovery journey.

Supporting Patient Care and Safety

To ensure the best possible care environment for our patients while maintaining hospital safety standards, we have established clear guidelines for all visitors These protocols help create a healing atmosphere and protect the well-being of patients, staff, and visitors alike. Please carefully review and follow these essential Do's and Don'ts during your visit to support your loved one's recovery journey.

Do`s

  • Obtain permission from the hospital staff before bringing any items for the patient.
  • Follow all safety protocols and guidelines set by the hospital during your visit.
  • Wash and sanitize your hands before and after visiting a patient.
  • Speak in low tones to maintain a calm and quiet atmosphere in patient areas.
  • Dispose of waste, including tissues and disposable items, in designated bins.
  • Wear protective gear, such as masks or gowns, when advised by hospital staff.
  • Be considerate of other patients and their families by keeping conversations minimal in shared spaces.
  • Inform hospital staff if you notice anything unusual about the patient's condition.
  • Encourage and support the patient's recovery with positive interactions and gestures.
  • Respect the hospital's visiting hours and leave when requested by staff.

Don`ts

  • Do not touch or move any medical equipment or supplies in the patient's room.
  • Avoid sitting on the patient's bed to prevent the spread of germs.
  • Do not bring small children into patient areas unless absolutely necessary.
  • Refrain from engaging in loud or disruptive activities during your visit.
  • Do not share personal items like phones, water bottles, or utensils with the patient.
  • Avoid congregating in hallways or blocking emergency pathways.
  • Do not enter restricted or staff-only areas without prior authorization.
  • Refrain from offering any medical advice or questioning the patient's prescribed treatment.
  • Do not visit the hospital if you are experiencing symptoms of illness that could be contagious.
  • Avoid bringing outside food, flowers, or gifts without prior approval from hospital staff.
