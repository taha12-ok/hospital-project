For our inpatients, staying at Radhe Krishna Hospital typically involves an overnight stay or longer. Here's essential information to help prepare for and enhance your hospital experience.

Admission Process

Upon confirmation from our scheduling department, you will be provided with the exact date and time for your hospital admission. On the day of arrival, please check in at reception where you'll be guided to your room. Our team will provide you with a patient wristband containing your personal information for seamless identification and enhanced safety throughout your care. Upon admission, our staff will also discuss your post-stay plans to ensure comprehensive aftercare. We will inquire about your home situation and recovery expectations to tailor your discharge plan to best support your healing journey.

What to Bring

  • Personal items
  • Comforting items (preferably starchy ones), small amounts of cash or ATM cards
  • Personal hygiene items (e.g., toothpaste, soap, etc.)
  • Important records and documents
  • Identification (passport, identity card)
  • Health insurance details
  • Pre-examination documents (e.g., X-ray reports, medical reports) and any relevant medical records (e.g., blood group, vaccination records, allergies, or diabetes records)
  • Medications that you take, with original packaging available
